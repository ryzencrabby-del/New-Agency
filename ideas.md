**Design Movement:** Dark Brutalism meets Digital Noir
**Core Principles:**
- High-contrast dark backgrounds with sharp white typography
- Asymmetric grid layouts with deliberate tension

- Raw, confident, no-fluff visual language

**Color Philosophy:** Near-black (#0D0D0F) base with white text and electric violet accents. The darkness signals authority and premium positioning; violet is the digital pulse of the brand.

- Thin violet horizontal rule lines as section dividers
- Bold oversized section numbers (01, 02, 03) in muted opacity
- Glowing violet CTA buttons with blur halos

**Interaction Philosophy:** Hover states reveal depth — cards lift with shadow, buttons pulse, nav links underline with a violet slide.

**Animation:** Entrance fade-up on scroll, staggered list reveals, subtle parallax on hero.

**Typography System:** Syne (display/headings, bold geometric) + DM Sans (body, clean readable). Headings at 5xl–7xl with tight tracking.

## Selected Design: Dark Brutalism meets Digital Noir (Option 1)

This design philosophy best suits a digital agency that wants to project confidence, technical authority, and premium positioning. The dark base with electric violet accents creates a memorable, distinctive brand identity that stands out from the sea of white-background agency sites.
